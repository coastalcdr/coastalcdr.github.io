ABOUT

As an Air Force veteran and spouse of a retired Air Force Veteran, I have travelled the world and worked in many areas of finance, compliance, licensing, research, technology, digital strategy, and design. I excel at applying a multidisciplinary approach utilizing my wide range of skillsets, helping brands streamline and refine their processes, and creating compelling, efficient, and creative solutions that galvanize success and improve operations. Helping businesses succeed, improve operations, and building longterm relationships and partnerships a passion of mine.

I am a huge sports fan, born and raised in New England,the Pats, Celtics, and Red Sox are who you'll find me rooting for. In my free time, you'll find me either spending time with my husband, kids and spoiled Pomeranian, drinking far too much coffee, refinishing furniture, baking, listening to music, snapping pics, traveling, or all of the above.

To further my growth as a developer, I am enrolled in a bootcamp program at PDX Code Guild and am currently pursuing my Master's Degree in Computer Science
